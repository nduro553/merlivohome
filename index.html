Our Journey

2018 - The Beginning

MerlivoHome was founded by a group of skilled craftsmen who recognized the growing need for professional furniture assembly services. Starting with just three technicians and a small service area, we focused on delivering exceptional quality and customer service that would set us apart.

2019 - Expansion Phase

Due to overwhelming positive feedback and word-of-mouth referrals, we expanded our team to ten technicians and broadened our service coverage. We invested in specialized tools and training programs to handle increasingly complex furniture systems and commercial installations.

2020 - Innovation Year

2021 - Commercial Growth

MerlivoHome began partnering with local businesses and property management companies, providing bulk assembly services for apartment complexes and office buildings. Our reputation for reliability and efficiency opened new opportunities in the commercial sector.

2022 - Recognition

We received multiple customer service awards and achieved a milestone of 3500 completed projects. Our team grew to include specialized technicians for different furniture categories, ensuring expert knowledge for every type of assembly.

2023 - Present Day

Today, MerlivoHome stands as a trusted name in furniture assembly services. We continue to invest in our team's training and development, staying ahead of industry trends and maintaining the high standards that our customers expect and deserve.

Our Areas of Expertise

Complex Modular Systems

We excel at assembling intricate modular furniture systems that require precise alignment and coordination. Our technicians understand the engineering behind these systems and ensure each module connects seamlessly for structural integrity and aesthetic appeal.

Flat-Pack Furniture

From simple bookcases to elaborate wardrobe systems, we handle all types of flat-pack furniture with efficiency. Our experience with various manufacturers means we can quickly interpret instructions and identify the most effective assembly sequence.

Custom Installations

When standard assembly isn't enough, we offer custom installation solutions. This includes wall mounting, creating built-in appearances, and adapting furniture to unique spaces or specific requirements.
